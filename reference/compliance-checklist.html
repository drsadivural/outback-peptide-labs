Compliance Questions Checklist

Internal working document · NOT part of the public website — do not link publicly

How to use this. This is a list of questions to take to a qualified Australian regulatory lawyer and/or a TGA compliance consultant, plus an accountant for the business items. It is not legal advice and is not exhaustive — it is a starting agenda so you get value from your first paid meeting. Resolve Section 1 and 2 before spending money on hosting, stock or payments.

Bring to your adviser

  • The full product list: BPC-157, Retatrutide, CJC-1295 + Ipamorelin, GHK-Cu, MOTS-c, Klow (BPC-157 + GHK-Cu + TB-500 + KPV), Tirzepatide, Selank, Semax.
  • Your intended customers (general public? registered researchers? institutions?).
  • Where you intend to source/import from, and where you'll ship.
  • This website (product copy, "research use only" framing, age gate).

1. Product classification & scheduling

  • What is the current Poisons Standard (SUSMP) schedule for each product? Which are Schedule 4 (prescription-only), and are any Schedule 9/10 or otherwise prohibited?
  • Does supplying any of these to the public require a prescription or a specific authority?
  • Does the "research use only / not for human consumption" framing actually change the legal classification, or is it treated as supply of a therapeutic good regardless?
  • Are any products captured as unapproved therapeutic goods under the Therapeutic Goods Act 1989 (Cth) if presented for sale to consumers?
  • How does the multi-peptide "Klow" blend affect classification (it contains TB-500 and KPV in addition to BPC-157 and GHK-Cu)?

2. Licences, permits & who you may supply

  • Do I need a licence to supply, wholesale or store these (e.g. State/Territory poisons or scheduled-substance licensing)?
  • Can I lawfully sell to the general public at all, or only to bona-fide research institutions with documented authority?
  • What buyer verification (research credentials, institutional accounts, end-use declarations) is legally required or advisable?
  • Are there manufacturing/labelling/GMP obligations that attach to how the vials are produced and labelled?

3. Advertising & claims

  • Does the Therapeutic Goods Advertising Code prohibit advertising these substances to the public (advertising prescription-only substances to consumers is generally not permitted)?
  • Are the current product descriptions (e.g. "metabolic research", "tissue-repair models") acceptable, or do they still imply prohibited therapeutic claims?
  • What must the mandatory disclaimers say and where must they appear?
  • Are testimonials, before/after content, or "best seller/popular" badges a compliance risk?

4. Importation & supply chain

  • Can these be lawfully imported into Australia? Do the Customs (Prohibited Imports) Regulations or an import permit apply?
  • What documentation must my supplier provide (COA, source, GMP)?
  • What are my obligations if a product is seized or a shipment is stopped?

5. Age restriction & customer verification

  • Is a self-declared 18+ browser gate sufficient, or is verified age/ID at checkout required?
  • What identity/eligibility records must I keep, and for how long?

6. Payments & banking

  • Will Stripe / PayPal / Square / my bank accept this business under their acceptable-use policies, or is it a prohibited/high-risk category likely to be frozen?
  • Do I need a high-risk merchant account, and what are the reserve/chargeback terms?
  • What happens to customer funds and orders if a processor terminates my account?

7. Business structure, tax & insurance

  • Best structure (sole trader / company) given the liability profile of these products?
  • ABN, GST registration threshold, and how GST applies to these sales.
  • What insurance is available/required (product liability, public liability) — and will an insurer cover this product category?

8. Privacy & data

  • Does the Privacy Act 1988 apply to me (turnover threshold, or health-information handling)?
  • Is my Privacy Policy adequate for the data I collect (including any ID verification)?
  • Notifiable Data Breach obligations if customer data is exposed.

9. Consumer law & fulfilment

  • Are my Terms, Refund and Shipping policies compliant with the Australian Consumer Law?
  • Are my "research use only" restrictions enforceable, and do they conflict with consumer guarantees?
  • Cooling-off / cancellation obligations, if any.

10. Record-keeping, recalls & adverse events

  • What sales/supply records must I keep (who bought what, when, verification)?
  • What are my obligations if a product must be recalled or an adverse event is reported?
  • Who is my point of contact / responsible person for regulator enquiries?

Where to get help

  • TGA — therapeutic goods regulation: tga.gov.au
  • Poisons Standard (SUSMP) — scheduling: search "Poisons Standard" on the Federal Register of Legislation.
  • A regulatory/health-law solicitor or a TGA compliance consultant for Sections 1–4.
  • An accountant for Section 7; the OAIC (oaic.gov.au) for privacy.
